Bedrock Streaming, which serves major European broadcasters including M6+, Videoland, and RTL+, employs a comprehensive FinOps strategy to reduce cloud spending. The company runs Kubernetes entirely on EC2 Spot Instances, adopts AWS Graviton processors for 15-20% savings on managed services, and reduced Amazon ECR costs by 90% through automated image purging. Key architectural changes eliminated inter-availability zone transfer costs, and a FinOps culture embedded across all teams ensures each percentage point of optimization generates considerable savings against multimillion-dollar annual budgets.
