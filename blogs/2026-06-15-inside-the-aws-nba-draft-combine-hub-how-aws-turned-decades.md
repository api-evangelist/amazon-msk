AWS and West Loop Strategy created an interactive data hub leveraging Amazon QuickSight to present 25+ years of NBA Draft Combine data. The serverless analytics platform helps fans understand how prospect measurements and performance metrics compare across positions and historical draft classes, enabling context-driven exploration rather than isolated metric lookups.
