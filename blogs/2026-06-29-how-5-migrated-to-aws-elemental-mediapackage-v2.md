Channel 5 (part of Paramount) consolidated its dual-pipeline packaging infrastructure onto AWS Elemental MediaPackage v2, eliminating manual failover and expanding to new platforms. The 12-month migration included DVB-DASH accessibility signaling, HLS manifest attributes, SCTE-35 flexibility, and SPEKEv2 DRM integration, consolidating a stack that had run eight EC2 instances and five additional MediaLive channels.
